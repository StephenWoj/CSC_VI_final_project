What is EquipClean

EquipClean is an APP for scheduling cleaning the gym equipment at the local health club.

The employee enters the cleaning tasks to keep track of their scheduled assignments. The tasks can be deleted when they are complete. Employees can also add updates to change their schedule.

This APP is for exclusive individual employee's tasks for which they are expected to manage. Management will then have confidence all the tasks are completed in a timely manner keeping the health club clean for all the guests.
